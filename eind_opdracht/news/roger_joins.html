Roger Daltrey to join rock legends cruise VII in 2019

Created on: 15 Mar 2018

On the heels of disembarking from their sixth sold-out voyage, Rock Legends Cruise (RLC) today announced that rock icon Roger Daltrey will join Rock Legends Cruise VII, February 14-18, 2019. Buddy Guy, Dave Davies and Kansas have also been confirmed to perform aboard the largest rock festival at sea.

The cruise is nearly sold out. A limited amount of cabins still available for purchase, visit the web site here or call 888-666-1499.

As a founding member of The Who and a solo artist, Roger Daltrey has voiced some of rock & roll’s most enduring anthems including, Who Are You, Won’t Get Fooled Again, I Can See for Miles, Pinball Wizard, Love, Reign O’er Me, and After the Fire. He is one of the most influential and awarded rock artists of the 20th century with honours including the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award, and a Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE).
